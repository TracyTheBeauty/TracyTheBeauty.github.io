“我们好像只是短暂地全球化了一下。”

‘We seems to only globalized briefly for a moment.’ ——硬汉拿铁

有一个双层的围墙环绕着中国——第一层由来自מִגְדַּל בָּבֶל的砖石筑成，一座语言的墙‬‬‎；第二层是防火长城，一座赛博的墙。希望这个博客能安抚中西学术轻微的断裂，互相平衡。

There is a double-layered wall around China: the first layer is made of masonry from מִגְדַּל בָּבֶל, a wall of language; the second layer is the Great Firewall, a wall of cyber. Hope this blog will soothe the slight rupture in East-West scholarship and counterbalance.

这同时也是一个私人博客。绝大部分翻译由我一人完成，我也偶然会上传一些我自己的（学术或文艺的）创作。写下这则概述时我是一名高二学生，只因为接触了进步的资源希望能与大家分享红利。投稿与斧正请以邮件联系我！

This is also a private blog. Almost all the translations are done by me alone, and I will occasionally upload some of my own (academic or artistic) works. I’m a sophomore in high school at the time of writing this overview, simply because of the exposure to advanced resources I hope to share the melons with everyone. Contact me by email for submissions and error corrections!

8.13 人文翻译计划 Humanities Translation Project

英文世界有若干相当优秀的人文学科的文献，适合本科学生与学术爱好者阅读；而简中世界也有许多奇异的文本与思想尚待发掘。我根据一些网站（如这个）和自己的阅读经验整理了这个持续更新的列表，并试图互相翻译成对方的语言。

The English world have several good articles, books and essays in the field of humanities that is suitable for undergraduate students and academic enthusiasts to read; and there are as well brilliant texts and ideas yet to be discovered in China. I have compiled this continously updating list based on a number of websites (like this one) and my own reading experiences, and have attempted to translate them into each other’s languages.

**

**

值得注意的是，考虑到翻译的初衷，无论是对于英语还是中文我都将尽量使用极直白的语言。这不可避免地增长了句子，或偶然增添了一些可能不完美但大致有效的内容。以下列表中，有一些文本我在网上找到了对应的翻译，我也都一应贴上链接供大家阅读了。如果得到了授权，我也会搬运到本站；但如非特殊注明，此站的翻译大多没有经过授权，翻译仅用于学术交流。请不要复制或者引用这些翻译，无论是出于学术目的还是商业目的。版权均归原作者所有，我放弃我的翻译的（实际从未有过的）版权。有一些文章可能是我的朋友或者我和我的朋友一同翻译的，但也请遵从上述的规则。

It’s worth noting that, given the original intent of the translation, I’ve attempted to use extremely plain language for both English and Chinese. This inevitably lengthened sentences, or occasionally adds something that may not be perfect but is presumably valid. There are some, however, translations have already done by someone else on the Internet, and I would still attach corresponding links for you to read. If authorized and needed, I might copy them to this website; and if not specially noted, are mostly unofficially translated (for in the case of a published official translation, I recommend you to purchase), and are for academic reference only. Please do not copy or cite from those translations for either (formal) academic and commercial uses. The copyright is retained by the original author, and I abjure the never-existed copyright of translation. Some of them might be translated by my friends or by me and my friends, and the rules shall be parallel.

  11. Quine, “On What There Is”
